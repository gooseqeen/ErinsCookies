Q and A

You have questions, and we have answers!

Here are some of our most frequently asked questions:

Why did you make the website in addition to your digital cookie website?

The reason why we made a website is because we wanted our website to answer users questions, and for it to be basically an attachment or an extension to the Digital Cookie website.

Why are you just not going to people's houses and giving them cookies?

The reason why is because covid is still happening so we don't know if we should be going to go to people’s houses. I just think it will be easier selling cookies on our website.

What is your goal with selling cookies?

My goal with cookie selling is to be able to reach 150 packages. The reason why I want to reach my goal is because last year I didn’t reach my goal, so this year I really want to sell cookies.

What’s the best thing about selling girl scout cookies?

The best part of cookie selling is seeing people happy and maybe trying something new, and falling in love with it. Another reason why I absolutely love cookie selling is seeing how much more I can sell each year and learning how to be a leader.

Why did Caitlin code the website instead of Erin?

Hey, Caitlin here. We both had a bit of a rough cookie season last year, and wanted to sell more cookies this year than ever before, so we decided to team up. My troup wasn't selling cookies, so I worked extra hard on helping my sister by coding. I may have coded and designed certain features of the website, but Erin made all of the major and small decisions for us. Hopefully one year I will be able to teach what I've learned, and we'll make the website together!

This may or may not need to be said, but we do not owngirl scout cookies. We are just girl scouts looking to reach customers with our own website.
